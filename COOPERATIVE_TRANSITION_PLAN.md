# Montessori Skye View Learning Center → Cooperative Transition Plan

## 📋 Executive Summary

This document outlines the comprehensive transition of Montessori Skye View Learning Center from a traditional private school model to a **parent cooperative model** where families are actively involved in the day-to-day operations, governance, and educational activities of the learning community.

## 🎯 Vision Statement

**"Transforming from a learning center to a collaborative educational community where parents, children, and educators work together to create an enriching Montessori environment that fosters independence, community engagement, and shared responsibility."**

## 🔄 Transition Overview

### Current State: Learning Center
- Traditional teacher-led Montessori education
- Parents as customers/clients
- Professional staff handles all operations
- Limited parent involvement beyond pickup/dropoff

### Target State: Parent Cooperative
- Collaborative parent-educator partnership
- Parents as active community members
- Shared responsibility for operations and governance
- Regular parent participation in classroom and administrative activities
